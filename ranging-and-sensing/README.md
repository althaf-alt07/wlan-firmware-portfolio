# Wi-Fi Ranging & Sensing

Experience working on Wi-Fi based positioning and sensing features across multiple standards.

---

## Supported Standards

- IEEE 802.11mc (Wi-Fi RTT)
- IEEE 802.11az (Next Generation Positioning)

---

## Key Areas of Work

### Round Trip Time (RTT)

- Implementation support for fine timing measurement based ranging
- Handling RTT measurement exchanges between initiator and responder
- Timing synchronization and measurement accuracy improvements
- Debugging ranging failures across firmware and host layers

---

### 802.11az Enhancements

- Exposure to next-generation ranging improvements
- Enhanced timing measurement flows
- Multi-AP positioning concepts

---

### Channel Frequency Response (CFR)

- Collection and processing of CFR data
- Support for sensing and location analytics
- Debugging signal quality and channel characteristics

---

## System Responsibilities

- Firmware control path handling for ranging procedures
- Interaction with MAC/PHY layers for measurement exchanges
- Telemetry collection for ranging performance evaluation
- Debugging ranging failures and timing inconsistencies

---

## Debug & Validation

- Timing accuracy analysis
- Measurement failure triage
- Cross-team debugging with PHY and system teams
- Validation under different RF environments
